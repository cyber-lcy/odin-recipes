Garlic Pasta

A pasta to keep away your coworkers!

Simple but awesome recipe with just 5 ingredients.

Better skip this recipe if you're a Vampire, you wont survive!

Ingredients

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • 50g Garlic
  • 40ml Olive Oil
  • Hand full of Parsley
  • Chili as you like
  1. Cook the pasta as usual
  2. Mince the garlic and parsley and put it into a pan on low heat with oliver oil
  3. Dry the pasta and give it into the pan, add some salt, pepper and chili and mix it
